ABOUT US

Driving innovation in ultra-high-speed transportation

SRM Club Hyperloopin is a student-led research and development initiative focused on advancing hyperloop technology. We are committed to developing the next generation of ultra-fast, sustainable transportation systems that will revolutionize how people and cargo move across the globe.

Our team consists of passionate engineering students of SRMIST from various disciplines working together to tackle the complex challenges of hyperloop technology. From aerodynamics and propulsion to magnetic levitation, from vacuum systems to developing software drives and systems, we explore every aspect of this revolutionary transportation concept.

Through hands-on research, prototype development, and collaboration with industry partners, we aim to contribute meaningfully to the future of transportation while providing our members with invaluable experience in cutting-edge engineering.
